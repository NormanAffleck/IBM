Management Information Systems (MIS)

Course Overview and Key Concepts

Course Description

This course introduces the principles and practices of Management Information Systems. Students will explore how information systems are used in business to improve operations, support decision-making, and create competitive advantage.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the role of information systems in organizations
  • Learn key MIS concepts such as data, information, and knowledge
  • Explore business applications like ERP, CRM, and SCM
  • Evaluate the impact of emerging technologies (AI, IoT, Big Data)
  • Analyze ethical and security issues in information systems

Key Topics

  • Introduction to MIS
  • Data Management and Databases
  • Enterprise Systems (ERP, CRM)
  • E-Commerce and Digital Business
  • Information Security and Ethics
  • Decision Support and Business Intelligence
  • Systems Development and Project Management
  • Artificial Intelligence and Automation
