🎯 Customer-Driven Strategy

  • STP Process: Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning
  • Steps: Identify market segments → Select targets → Position product

🔍 Market Segmentation

  • Segment Consumer, Business, and International markets
  • Segmentation types: Geographic, Demographic, Psychographic, Behavioral
  • Effective segments: Measurable, Accessible, Substantial, Differentiable, Actionable

🎯 Market Targeting

  • Target market = buyers with shared needs the firm chooses to serve
  • Targeting strategies: Undifferentiated, Differentiated, Concentrated, Micromarketing
  • Examples: Marriott Hotels, Starbucks, iPhone models, SSG Food, LCC Airlines

🧩 Differentiation and Positioning

  • Product Positioning: Defined by consumers' perception on key attributes
  • Developing competitive advantage: Must be Important, Distinctive, Profitable, etc.
  • Positioning Map: Visual tool to compare perception of brands
  • Value Proposition = Full benefit mix used to position the brand

✅ Conclusion

Chapter 7 emphasizes designing strategies that revolve around customer segmentation, targeting specific market needs, and positioning products to gain sustainable competitive advantage in a dynamic market.
