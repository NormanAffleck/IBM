Marketing Mix (4Ps)

The marketing mix includes four essential elements a firm uses to implement its marketing strategy:

  • Product: What the firm offers to meet customer needs.
  • Price: The cost customers pay for the product.
  • Place: Where and how the product is distributed and sold.
  • Promotion: How the product is advertised and promoted to target customers.
