Chapter 5 | Front Desk

🎯 Objectives

  • Understand the main functions of the Front Office Department

🏨 Room Division Overview

  • Departments: Front Office, Housekeeping, Reservation, Bell/Concierge
  • Subsections: Night Audit, Executive Lounge, Business Center, Tour Desk

👥 Key Room Division Roles

  • Room Division Director – oversees entire division
  • Front Office Manager – supervises front operations, customer care, HR
  • Manager – intermediary communication and customer control
  • Assistant Manager/Supervisor – operations support, shift duty, log checks
  • Clerk – direct customer service

📋 Workflow: Guest Journey

  • Before Arrival – Reservation
  • Arrival – Door/Bell Desk, Concierge
  • Room Assignment – Front Desk
  • In-House Stay – Room Division supports
  • Check-Out – Front Desk
  • Departure – Door/Bell/Concierge assist

🛎️ Front Desk Functions

  • Customer reception and farewell
  • FOH (Front of House) operations
  • Revenue center – upselling, room sales

📝 Check-in Procedures

  • Reservation check – guest info and ID
  • Walk-in guest handling and upselling
  • Registration card – contact, ID, passport, signature
  • Deposit – cash or credit card (pre-authorization)
  • Room assignment – smoking preference, length of stay, rate
  • Room key issuance and final confirmation

📦 Other Front Desk Services

  • Delivery of guest goods (fruit, wine, messages)
  • Valuables deposit and Lost & Found
  • Currency exchange – floating or fixed rates

📤 Check-out Process

  • Room charge and key return
  • Feedback and farewell
  • System checkout and receipt

🌃 Night Audit

  • Shift overview – Morning, Afternoon, Night
  • Daily balance – cash, credit card deposits
  • No-show and room status reports
  • Closing the day, opening next

👑 Executive Floor

  • VIP check-in/check-out
  • Late check-out, breakfast, meeting room
  • Lounge and personalized services

💼 Business Center

  • Secretarial service
  • Office equipment, meeting space
