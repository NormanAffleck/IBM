Week 11 | Menu Management

📋 Menu Definition

  • List of food and beverages served in a restaurant
  • Shapes restaurant image and dining experience
  • Acts as a marketing tool and promise to customers

🍽️ Menu Types

  • By dining time: Breakfast, Brunch, Lunch, Afternoon Tea, Dinner
  • By meal type: Table d’hote, A La Carte, Buffet

📝 Menu Planning

  • Critical to restaurant operation and success
  • Considers item selection, cost/profit, nutrition, availability, cook’s skill, and equipment

📊 Menu Engineering

  • Analyzes Popularity vs. Profitability of items
  • Contribution margin = Selling price - Variable cost
  • Menu mix analysis:
    • 🌟 Star: High popularity + High profitability
    • 🐄 Cash Cow: High popularity + Low profitability
    • 🧩 Puzzle: Low popularity + High profitability
    • 🐶 Dog: Low popularity + Low profitability

💰 Menu Item Pricing

  • Factors: cost, competition, and demand
  • Cost-oriented: Price = Cost + Margin
  • Competition-oriented: Pricing based on market
  • Demand-oriented: Adjust to customer willingness

🎨 Menu Design

  • Framing: Use of $ symbol, rounding (e.g., 19.99 vs. 20.00)
  • Understandability: Categories, photos, language, and names
  • Menu layout: Placement and scan pattern influence choices
