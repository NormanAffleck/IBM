Chapter 7 | Housekeeping

🎯 Objectives

  • Understand major responsibilities of the Housekeeping department

🏨 Housekeeping Overview

  • Maintains cleanliness of rooms and hotel facilities
  • Manages fixed assets and building infrastructure
  • Core function of room product production

🔧 Key Housekeeping Functions

  • Ensure room cleanliness and pleasantness
  • Manage buildings and facilities
  • Reduce maintenance costs by managing fixed assets
  • Internal communication with other hotel departments
  • Enhance guest satisfaction and safety

🧹 Housekeeping Tasks

  • Room preparation and turndown service
  • Room inspection and reporting to Front Desk
  • Room amenities setup
  • Handling special requests and communication

📋 Room Status Terminology

  • Clean and Vacant, Occupied, On-Change
  • Do Not Disturb, Sleep-out, Stay-over
  • Due Out, Checkout, Out of Order, Lock Out, Late Checkout

🧼 Public Area & Linen Management

  • Clean public areas (lobby, restroom, pool, etc.)
  • Handle linen: collect, classify, wash, dry, fold, store, deliver

🧺 Other Services

  • In-room guest service
  • Lost & Found management
  • Room damage reports, DNS (Do Not Sales), Out of Service

📘 Key Terminology

  • Room Inspector, Room Attendant
  • Linen (sheets, covers), Terry (towels)
  • Room Attendant Cart, OPL (On-premise laundry)
  • Par Level – minimum required linen for operation
