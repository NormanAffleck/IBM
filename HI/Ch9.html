A business specializing in food preparation and service: restaurants, delivery, catering, etc. Regulated by the Food Service Industry Promotion Act.

  • Inseparability, labor intensive

  • Small volume, multi-kind production
  • Uncertainty in demand
  • Seasonality and high employee turnover
  • Modern trends: instant food, delivery growth, health-conscious, brand preference

üìä Classification of Food Service Industry

  • By management format: direct, consignment, franchise

üç¥ Classification of Restaurants
