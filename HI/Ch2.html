Chapter 2 | Hospitality Service

📖 Definition of Service

  • Activity or benefit offered for sale
  • Non-physical product
  • Intangible, perishable, inseparable, variable

🏨 Characteristics of Hospitality Service

  • Intangibility: cannot be seen, touched, or measured before delivery
  • Perishability: cannot be stored for future use
  • Inseparability: production and consumption occur simultaneously
  • Variability: quality depends on who provides it, when, where, how

🧠 Consumer Behavior in Service

  • High customer participation and interaction
  • Experience-driven perception
  • Subjective satisfaction

📋 Service Blueprinting

  • Mapping service processes
  • Identifying customer touchpoints
  • Helps maintain quality and standardization

🔧 Quality Control Strategies

  • Employee training
  • Service manuals
  • Standard operating procedures (SOPs)

🙋‍♀️ Empowerment & Service Recovery

  • Frontline staff authorized to resolve issues
  • Recovering dissatisfied customers builds loyalty
  • Encourages initiative, builds trust and response time
