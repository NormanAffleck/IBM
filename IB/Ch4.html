Week 4 | Hotel Products

🌟 What Is a Hotel Product?

The final product a hotel delivers is a memorable hospitality service — a wonderful hotel stay that’s comfortable, safe, and enjoyable.

🧬 Characteristics of Hotel Product

  • Intangible
  • Inseparability
  • Heterogeneity
  • Perishability
  • Repetitiveness

🛏️ Guestroom as Core Product

Guestrooms contribute the largest revenue share — about 68.1% on average, with up to 99.1% in limited-service hotels. They take up roughly 70% of the total hotel building area.

🔒 Guestroom Conditions

  • Safety
  • Pleasantness
  • Cleanliness
  • Convenience
  • Privacy
  • Quietness

🛋 Guestroom Functions

  • Sleep and rest
  • Personal freedom
  • Hygiene space

🛏️ Guestroom Classifications (1) – Bed Type

  • Single Room
  • Double Room (Double/Queen/King)
  • Twin Room (Single–Single)
  • Deluxe Twin
  • Family Twin
  • Ondol Room, Triple Room, Futon, Rollaway

🏞️ Guestroom Classifications (2) – Location

  • Outside Room
  • Inside Room
  • Adjoining Room
  • Connecting Room

🎖️ Guestroom Classifications (3) – Service Level

  • Standard
  • Superior
  • Deluxe
  • Executive Floor (EFL)
  • Suite Room (Royal, Presidential)

🎨 Guestroom Classifications (4) – Guest Preference

  • Smoking / Non-smoking
  • Handicap Room
  • Ladies Room
  • Theme / Character Room

🏗 Physical Components

  • Sleep: Bed, Sofa, Bedside Table
  • Sanitary: Bathtub, Basin, Toilet
  • Office: Desk, Phone, Laptop
  • Storage: Closet, Drawers, Safe
  • Convenience: Minibar, TV, Kettle

💲 Room Rates

  • Rack Rate
  • Special Rates: Corporate, Group, Seasonal, Complimentary
  • Extra Charges: Late Checkout, Third Guest, Cancellation, Day Use
  • Special Rates: Employee, Package, Hold Room

🍽️ Auxiliary Hotel Products

  • Food & Beverage (avg. 25% of revenue)
  • Banquet, Meeting Facilities
  • Spa / Sauna, Fitness, Pool
  • Room Amenities, Free Wi-Fi

🚌 Additional Services

  • Activity Packages
  • Transportation / Shuttle / Limo Services
  • Tour & Rental Products
