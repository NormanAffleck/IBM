Week 9 | Privacy and Security

📍 Location-Based Services (LBS)

  • Identify and transmit users’ location data via mobile devices
  • Provide personalized services using spatial and temporal patterns

📈 Value of Customer Data

  • Shifting to known audiences enhances personalized experiences
  • Personal data critical to communication, targeting, and service improvement
  • Risks: misuse, leaks, ethical concerns

🔐 Privacy and Its Definitions

  • "The right to be let alone" — both physical and information privacy
  • Information privacy: Control over how one’s data is collected and used
  • Privacy paradox: People express concern, yet act contrary (e.g., oversharing)

🌐 Privacy in the IoT World

  • Everyday objects collect/transmit data (smart devices)
  • Notice-and-consent principles are hard to enforce
  • Massive data collection often occurs without awareness

⚠️ Data Breaches

  • US: 422 million+ affected; Korea: 65 million+ affected
  • Corporate and personal data are both at risk

💤 Privacy Fatigue

  • Security, breach, and consent fatigue leads to inaction
  • Reduced satisfaction, reputation damage, and legal risks

🔓 Why Systems Are Vulnerable

  • Hardware/software failures, poor configuration, and natural disasters
  • Outside networks and portable device loss pose threats

🧨 Computer Crime & Threats

  • DDoS attacks, botnets, spam, and phishing
  • Computers can be both targets and tools of crime

👥 Insider Threats

  • Users and staff may create risks due to negligence or poor training

🔧 Security Controls

  • General: Design and security policies
  • Application: Input, processing, output safeguards

📉 Risk & Disaster Planning

  • Risk assessments calculate loss probabilities and expected impact
  • Plans for recovery and business continuity are essential

📋 Security Audits

  • Simulate disasters and test firm’s response capabilities
  • Rank vulnerabilities and prepare countermeasures

🔐 Key Security Tools

  • Authentication (passwords, biometrics, smart cards)
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Antivirus and anti-spyware software

☁️ Security in the Cloud

  • Data owner must ensure cloud provider compliance
  • Important: location, separation, legal standards, certifications

📊 Business Impact of Security

  • Security breaches harm reputation and market value
  • Ransomware and lack of controls can lead to major financial loss
