Week 3 | Competitive Advantage in MIS

🏛 The Relationship Between Organizations & IT

IT and organizations influence each other through structure, culture, politics, and managerial decisions. Organizational success depends on how well technology is aligned with internal and external dynamics.

📐 What Is an Organization?

  • Formal structure converting inputs (labor, capital) to outputs
  • Social structure balancing responsibilities and rights over time
  • Microeconomic system of resource transformation with feedback loops

🏗 Organizational Features & Business Processes

  • Hierarchical structures, decision accountability, efficiency focus
  • Standardized routines form business processes that drive goals
  • Culture and politics shape operations and resistance to change

🏛️ Types of Organizational Structures (Mintzberg)

  • Entrepreneurial (startups)
  • Machine bureaucracy (mass production)
  • Divisional bureaucracy (Fortune 500)
  • Professional bureaucracy (law firms, hospitals)
  • Adhocracy (flexible task-force teams)

📉 Economic & Behavioral Impact of IT

  • Reduces transaction and agency costs
  • Flattens organizations — less hierarchy, more autonomy
  • Improves decision speed, productivity, and span of control

🛡 Porter’s Five Forces

  • Traditional competitors
  • New entrants
  • Substitute products/services
  • Customer bargaining power
  • Supplier bargaining power

🧠 Strategic IS Approaches

  • Low-Cost Leadership: Use IT to cut costs and increase operational efficiency (e.g., Walmart)
  • Product Differentiation: New services and mass customization (e.g., Nike iD)
  • Market Niche: Analyze data to target specific segments
  • Customer/Supplier Intimacy: Personalized services, smart supply chains

🌐 Internet & Smart Products

The internet intensifies competition but offers new digital business models and smarter, IoT-enabled products that raise switching costs and reshape industries.

📊 Competitive Advantage with Data

  • Smart products expand differentiation, raise customer loyalty
  • Data Literacy is crucial for making informed decisions in the digital era
  • ABC of the 4th Industrial Revolution: AI, Big Data, Cloud Computing
